ctsTraffic.exe -target:localhost -protocol:udp -bitspersecond:8000000 -framerate:30 -bufferdepth:10 -streamlength:30 -connections:1 -statusupdate:500 -iterations:1 -consoleverbosity:5 -JitterFilename:simpletestjitter.csv


100Gbps with lower bits/sec per connection, but higher connection count:
ctsTraffic.exe -listen:* -protocol:udp -bitspersecond:100000000 -FrameRate:100 -StreamLength:30 -ConsoleVerbosity:1 -ServerExitLimit:400
ctsTraffic.exe -target:localhost -protocol:udp -bitspersecond:100000000 -FrameRate:100 -BufferDepth:3 -StreamLength:30 -iterations:1 -ConsoleVerbosity:1 -verify:data -connections:400
expecting 0% loss rate (on my Ryzen 7 5800X)

100Gbps with higher bits/sec per connection, but lower connection count:
ctsTraffic.exe -listen:* -protocol:udp -bitspersecond:1000000000 -FrameRate:100 -StreamLength:30 -ConsoleVerbosity:1 -ServerExitLimit:100
ctsTraffic.exe -target:localhost -protocol:udp -bitspersecond:1000000000 -FrameRate:100 -BufferDepth:3 -StreamLength:30 -iterations:1 -ConsoleVerbosity:1 -verify:connection -connections:100 
expecting < 0.2% drop rate (on my Ryzen 7 5800X)
